## 1. FAQ (Frequently Asked Questions)

### General Questions

**Q: Where is Water Nest located?**
A: Water Nest operates from Kathu, Northern Cape, South Africa. Our physical address is available upon request for local customers requiring in-person consultations.

**Q: What areas do you service?**
A: We primarily service the Northern Cape region, with particular focus on Kathu and surrounding mining communities. We also offer nationwide delivery throughout South Africa for our products.

**Q: What payment methods do you accept?**
A: We accept EFT (Electronic Funds Transfer), credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard), and cash on delivery for local orders in the Kathu area.

**Q: Do you offer installation services?**
A: Yes, we provide professional installation services for water filtration systems, purifiers, and treatment equipment within the Northern Cape region. Installation fees vary based on location and system complexity.

### Product Questions

**Q: Are your products SABS approved?**
A: Yes, all our water treatment products comply with South African Bureau of Standards (SABS) regulations and SANS 241 drinking water quality standards.

**Q: Do you offer water testing services?**
A: Yes, we provide comprehensive water testing services to assess water quality and recommend appropriate treatment solutions for your specific needs.

**Q: What warranty do your products carry?**
A: Most products carry a 12-24 month manufacturer's warranty. Extended warranties are available for purchase. Warranty terms comply with the Consumer Protection Act 68 of 2008.

**Q: Can I return a product if it doesn't meet my needs?**
A: Yes, in accordance with the Consumer Protection Act, you have a cooling-off period to return products. See our Returns Policy for full details.

### Order & Delivery Questions

**Q: How long does delivery take?**
A: Local Kathu deliveries: 1-3 business days. Northern Cape: 3-5 business days. Nationwide: 5-10 business days depending on courier service and location.

**Q: Do you charge for delivery?**
A: Delivery within Kathu town limits is free for orders over R1,500. Other areas incur courier charges based on weight and distance.

**Q: Can I track my order?**
A: Yes, once your order is dispatched, you'll receive a tracking number via email and SMS.

**Q: What if I'm not home when delivery arrives?**
A: Our courier partners will attempt delivery twice. You can also arrange collection from their depot or schedule a convenient delivery time.

## 3. Returns Policy

*In compliance with the Consumer Protection Act 68 of 2008*

### Your Rights

Under South African law, you have the right to:
- Return goods within the cooling-off period
- Return defective or unsafe goods
- Receive a refund, replacement, or repair for faulty goods

### Cooling-Off Period (Change of Mind)

**7-Day Return Window**
- You may return products within 7 days of delivery
- Products must be unused, in original packaging with all accessories
- You must pay return shipping costs
- Refund processed within 10 business days of receiving returned item
- 10% restocking fee applies

**Exclusions from Cooling-Off Period:**
- Custom-made or specially ordered items
- Products that have been installed or used
- Consumable products (filters, cartridges) if packaging is opened
- Water testing kits after seals are broken

### Defective or Faulty Products

**30-Day Quality Guarantee**
- Report defects within 30 days of delivery
- We'll collect the item at no cost to you (within Northern Cape)
- Choose replacement, repair, or full refund
- No restocking fee for defective items

**Warranty Claims**
- Manufacturer warranties: 12-24 months (product dependent)
- Keep your proof of purchase
- Warranties cover manufacturing defects, not wear and tear
- Professional installation required for some warranty validation

### How to Return an Item

1. **Contact Us**
   - Email: returns@waternest.co.za
   - Phone: 082 XXX XXXX
   - Provide order number and reason for return

2. **Receive Authorization**
   - We'll issue a Return Authorization (RA) number
   - You'll receive return instructions
   - For defective items, we arrange collection

3. **Pack the Item**
   - Use original packaging if possible
   - Include all accessories, manuals, and documentation
   - Include your RA number and invoice copy

4. **Ship the Item**
   - Use a trackable shipping method
   - Keep shipping receipt for insurance purposes
   - We recommend insuring high-value items

5. **Refund Processing**
   - Inspection completed within 3 business days of receipt
   - Refund processed within 10 business days
   - Refund via original payment method

### Non-Returnable Items

- Water that has been tested or dispensed
- Custom installations already completed
- Products damaged due to misuse or negligence
- Items without original packaging (unless defective)
- Electrical items with broken safety seals (unless defective)

### Damaged During Shipping

If your order arrives damaged:
- Don't accept delivery if visibly damaged
- If damage discovered after acceptance, contact us within 48 hours
- Take photos of packaging and damaged item
- We'll arrange replacement or refund immediately
- No cost to you for shipping-damaged items
